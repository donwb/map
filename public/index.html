Rewards Program Summary

The Cloud Migration Awards Program recognizes the exceptional work of the teams within GTO as we push forward in our pursiut of being a cloud first organization. Highlighting the achievements of those within the GTO Community, the awards program also hopes to inspire teams to embrace change as we move forward.
